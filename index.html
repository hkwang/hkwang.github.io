Harrison K. Wang

I'm a PhD student in the Biophysics graduate program at Harvard University, working in Professor Doeke Hekstra's lab on experimental dynamics of membrane proteins.

I'm broadly interested in protein dynamics—as in my undergraduate work studying tau and SARS-CoV-2 E protein using solid-state NMR in Professor Mei Hong's lab; and in structural biology methods—as in my rotation work in Professor Haribabu Arthanari 's lab studying chemical shift encoding in multidimensional NMR spectra (manuscript in preparation).

My research had been supported by the National Science Foundation Graduate Research Fellowship Program (2022-2025).
